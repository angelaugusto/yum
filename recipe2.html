Ginger Ale Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 3 lemons
  • 1 pint of ginger ale
  • 1Â½ pints of water
  • a cupful of sugar

Preparation

  1. Place a good sized piece of ice in a glass pitcher and pour over it the juice of the lemons, sugar and water, thoroughly mixed.
  2. Stir and when the sugar is dissolved add the ginger ale.
  3. This should be served without delay, while the fizz is still in the ginger ale.
